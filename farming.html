Farming Services

Farming involves a wide range of tasks that cover various stages of crop and livestock production. Here's a list of works that typically come under farming:

1. Land Preparation

  • Plowing, harrowing, and leveling fields.
  • Soil testing and fertilization.
  • Clearing land of debris and rocks.

2. Planting and Sowing

  • Seed selection and preparation.
  • Sowing seeds or transplanting seedlings.
  • Irrigation system setup and management.

3. Crop Maintenance

  • Watering and irrigation.
  • Fertilization and soil enrichment.
  • Weed control (manual or chemical).
  • Pest and disease management.
  • Pruning and thinning plants.

4. Harvesting

  • Gathering crops (manual or mechanical).
  • Post-harvest handling (sorting, cleaning, packing).
  • Transportation to storage or market.

5. Soil and Water Management

  • Soil conservation practices (terracing, crop rotation).
  • Building drainage systems and irrigation channels.
  • Rainwater harvesting and storage.

6. Livestock Farming

  • Feeding, breeding, and managing livestock.
  • Milking and dairy processing.
  • Veterinary care and health management.
  • Cleaning and maintaining pens and barns.

7. Post-Harvest Management

  • Drying, milling, and processing crops.
  • Storing in silos or warehouses.
  • Value addition through packaging and branding.

8. Farm Equipment Operation and Maintenance

  • Operating tractors, harvesters, and other machinery.
  • Regular maintenance and repairs of farm tools.
  • Fuel management.
